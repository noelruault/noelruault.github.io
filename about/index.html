About

My name is Noël Ruault,

Working since 2015 in the software industry, I started working as a Python programmer in a robotics company, gradually moving on to become an experienced Golang software engineer and systems designer.

At this stage of my professional life, I have just expanded my knowledge in cloud technologies. Recently I have been working with Kubernetes and I have learned to scale systems efficiently, which added to my previously acquired knowledge in backend and databases has given me a lot of confidence when it comes to having a whole software system under control.

One of my core guidelines is to work to strengthen my personal and professional skills and always keep the enthusiasm and willingness to learn. I identify myself as a software engineer with attention to detail, committed to testing my work, and compromised with delivery deadlines.

My colleagues often consider me a team player, outgoing, dedicated, and open-minded. Who gets along well with people and adapts to change easily.
